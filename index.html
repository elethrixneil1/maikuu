ABOUT MAIKU 99.5 FM

Broadcasting Excellence from the Heart of Bicol.

Located in the heart of Nabua, Maiku 99.5 FM is a dynamic and modern radio station dedicated to delivering the best in music, entertainment, news, and community connection across the Bicol Region. Broadcasting from Camarines Sur, the station serves listeners with a fresh mix of today’s hottest hits, local favorites, engaging programs, and interactive on-air experiences.

Driven by passion and innovation, Maiku 99.5 FM aims to become one of the leading voices in Bicol Region by providing quality entertainment and meaningful content that connects people through music and information. With its slogan, “Your Music. Your Station.”, Maiku 99.5 FM represents energy, creativity, and the true spirit of the local community.

Whether it’s music that inspires, stories that matter, or moments that bring listeners together, Maiku 99.5 FM is committed to being the soundtrack of everyday life in Bicol and beyond.
